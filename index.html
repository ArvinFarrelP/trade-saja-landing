🌸 Meet the Ensemble 🌸

Four characters with distinct personalities and dreams

Clara
No. 01 — The Perfectionist
A former fencing athlete who proves that being a maid is an art requiring high discipline. Behind her cool demeanor, she is the first to make sure all her friends are doing well.
Dream Professional Household Manager
Midori
No. 02 — The Wise Elder
A soft-hearted herbalist who became head maid because of her natural maternal nature. She has a sharp sense of humor and often gives advice while casually sweeping the floor.
Dream Medicinal Plant Encyclopedia Author
Sophie
No. 03 — The Sunshine
A talented street dancer who can't walk normally — she always moves as if dancing. Sophie is the "sun" of this house who often plays music in the kitchen every morning.
Dream Open a Free Dance School
Chloe
No. 04 — The Gentle Flower
A noble's daughter from a plant-loving family. She joined to learn how to manage a giant garden. Her blue eyes always sparkle whenever she sees roses blooming in the morning.
Dream Own a Flower Shop
